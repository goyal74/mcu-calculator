Supplementary tool for Goyal & Goyal 2026. Translate between DNA content (Mb), metaphase length (μm), MCUs, Hi-C boundaries, and cytogenetic detection thresholds.
Conversion constants used (this manuscript):
 • 1 MCU = 7.6 Mb DNA = 0.25 μm metaphase axial length
 • Linear compaction = 33.4 nm/Mb (95% CI [32.3, 33.5])
 • Abbe diffraction limit ≈ 196 nm at NA 1.4, λ 550 nm = 6.0 Mb threshold
 • 1 MCU contains ≈ 41 Rao 2014 contact domains, ≈ 9 Dixon 2012 TADs, ≈ 150 Gibcus mitotic loops
